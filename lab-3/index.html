About Volkswagen Polo

German automaker Volkswagen has been producing the B-segment supermini, or Polo, since 1975. Throughout its production run, hatchback, saloon, and estate versions are available in Europe and other markets across the world. There have been six distinct generations of the Polo created as of 2018, each distinguished by a "Series" or "Mark" number.

Midway through manufacturing, certain generations received a facelift; the modified versions were informally identified by adding the letter F to the mark number, such as Mk2F. Since certain enthusiasts and members of the automotive press view the facelifts as distinct models, they have unofficially given the earlier iterations of the Polo Mk1 through Mk7 titles.In addition, a two- or three-character Volkswagen Group Typ number is used to identify each Polo model.
